# Quantum Variational Algorithms

## Objective
To understand and implement variational quantum algorithms from first principles, focusing on VQE and QAOA.

## Roadmap

1. Introduction to VQAs
2. Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE)
3. Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA)

## Structure

- Introduction to VQA and VQE/
- QAOA/ (upcoming)

## Long-term goal

Develop a deep, practical understanding of VQAs and build reproducible implementations with clear theoretical grounding.
